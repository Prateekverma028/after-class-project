Pizza

a globally popular dish, is a baked flatbread, traditionally Italian, topped with various ingredients like cheese, tomato sauce, and toppings. It has roots in ancient flatbreads and evolved in Naples, Italy, with tomatoes becoming a key ingredient in the late 18th century. The word "pizza" was first documented in 997 AD in Gaeta, Italy, and is believed to originate from the Italian word "pinza," meaning "clamp" or "pliers,"
